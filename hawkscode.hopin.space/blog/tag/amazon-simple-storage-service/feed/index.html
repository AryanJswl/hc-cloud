In this blog, we will discuss about Ecommerce war Shopify vs Amazon. There has been a longstanding debate on Shopify vs Amazon and which e-commerce giant will be best-suited to your selling needs.

This battle can easily be regarded as the biggest in the world of e-commerce. In addition to that, it cannot be denied that both Shopify and Amazon are big names in present times as they provide massive opportunities for selling as well as buying online.

Shopify moves to challenge Amazon in the fulfillment business

Shopify is venturing into a segment of e-commerce that Amazon has long sought to dominate. Amazon operates a network of warehouses from which it handles fulfillment functions for its merchants. That way, Amazon focuses on product packaging and delivery time.

Amazon allows you to sell your products through its marketplace, where other online sellers do the same. But with Shopify you can build your own ecommerce store. You don’t need to pay any charges to amazon. To get a Ecommerce store visit this blog Online Shopping Store for your business.

The advantages of Shopify are as follows:

  • Resource-Oriented E-Commerce Tools: Shopify has a wide range of E-commerce tools that is designed to help you build awareness and boost your online store.
  • Your Own Unique Brand Identity:If you want to create your own E-commerce website, you want to create a strong and unique brand influence to keep up with your business.
  • Security and Reliability Another benefit of Shopify is the security and reliability it offers. Security is essential for an online business because it deals with confidential personal and financial information of the customers.

The drawbacks of Shopify are as follows:

  • Costly : Shopify has a monthly fees, but it also features and AppStore. The Implementation of the third party apps can boost your monthly costs.
  • Less Customizable : Shopify app store has a lot of very good functionalities, but it has no comparison to the ocean of plugins available for platforms like Woocommerce, a lot of which are free

The Advantages of Amazon are as follows:

  • Increased Traffic: It has been estimated that millions of people often visit the Amazon marketplace on monthly basis. This gives your products a great advantage and exposure to the consumer market.
  • Add-on Services: Amazon easily handles your inventory storage, packing and shipments for you, and it makes easier for you to promote your brand.

The drawbacks of Amazon are as follows:

  • Tough Competition: There is a fierce competition in the productive marketplace, Amazon sells millions of products, so you’re up against millions others in the competition. Sometimes that you’ll be directly competing with Amazon itself.
  • Brand Awareness:It seems a difficult task to devise a stunning brand image by selling products and services on Amazon.

In this blog you will get a idea about what is amazon web services and their products. Amazon Web Services (AWS) is a cloud service platform, offering computing power,storage ,database and other service to help business scale and grow. Cloud provides IT resources on Internet with pay-per-price. You need not to buy any physical storage or server instead you can take it on rent at a very cheap price. There is a huge list of products offer by AWS but the major one is EC2, Amazon’s virtual machine service and S3, Amazon’s storage system.

Amazon Elastic Computer Cloud EC2

Amazon Elastic Computer Cloud EC2 is a web service that provide securable, scalable computer capacity in cloud. EC2 eliminates the need to invest in hardware, So you can develop and deploy applications faster. EC2 is used to run virtual machine in cloud. AWS calls these virtual machine instances.

Amazon Simple Storage Service S3

Amazon Simple Storage Service S3 provides web storage through web service interface. S3 enables customers to upload, store and download practically any file or object that is up to five terabytes (TB) in size. It is durable for objects store in service and support multiple security. To learn more about AWS cloud visit Easyshiksha.

Save costs without sacrificing performance by storing data across the S3 Storage Classes, which support different data access levels at corresponding rates. You can use S3 Storage Class Analysis to discover data that should move to a lower-cost storage class based on access patterns, and configure an S3 Lifecycle policy to execute the transfer.
