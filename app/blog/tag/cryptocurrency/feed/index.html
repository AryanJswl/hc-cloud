As of 2017, price boom was a real happiness for all the investors and coin admins. But all of a sudden all the happiness just disappeared. Price of the coins was sky high in last Dec-Jan but then it starts falling.

Bitcoin, a famous cryptocurrency that reached at the height of $19,000 is not at $6,000. That is still better than the other cryptocurrencies. So many of these are not worthy of a cent now. Now due to decentralization, it is hard to track the company. Not only Bitcoin but other coins like Ethereum, Ripple has also shown the same fate as of bitcoin. These coins also made some hike of 5-15 percent of the previous price.
